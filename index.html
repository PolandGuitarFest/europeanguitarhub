For too long, European guitar players have overlooked the incredible craftsmanship happening right in their backyard. While overseas brands dominate the market, hundreds of talented European builders remain in the shadows.

European Guitar Hub changes that. We're creating a unified platform where players can discover, learn about, and connect with European-made gear - from guitars and basses to effects pedals, amplifiers, and everything in between.

Whether you're in Warsaw, Madrid, Milan, or anywhere across Europe, it's time to discover what's being built in your own continent.
